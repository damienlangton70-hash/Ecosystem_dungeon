# Deepforage — Key Art

North-star concept art for the Graphics & Styles agents to art-match against.
These are *targets for mood, palette, and lighting* — the shipping game is stylized
low-poly, but it should evoke this.

### What to match

- **Palette:** cold bioluminescence — teal, electric blue, violet — against **dark,
  desaturated stone**, punctuated by a single **warm amber** light source (lantern /
  campfire). Cold glow + warm flame is the whole game's signature contrast.
- **Lighting:** the glowcap caps and glow-fungus are the light sources; deep shadow
  everywhere else; volumetric haze for depth and scale.
- **Elements:** towering **glowcap pillar-trees** with broad luminescent caps, a still
  **bioluminescent water pool** that mirrors the canopy, scattered ground glow-fungus,
  and a **lone delver** silhouette for scale and loneliness.
- **Mood:** a beautiful, dangerous deep that trends ever downward. Awe first, threat close behind.
- **Style:** stylized low-poly with painterly atmosphere — readable silhouettes, chunky
  forms, colour and light doing the heavy lifting (see `../DESIGN_BIBLE.md` §7).

*Generated as an art target during M1. Replace or expand as the look develops.*
